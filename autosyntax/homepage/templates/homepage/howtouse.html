How to use

{# G E N R A L B E H A V I O U R#}
General behaviour

There are only 3 mechanisms in action:

An Operator must appear before the arguments (e.g list, def, class and so on).

An argument can be marked with a dot, to toggle a certain behaviour

A built-in method that precedes an argument, is applied to that argument


For more details, click here.
