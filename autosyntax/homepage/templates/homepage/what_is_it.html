What is it?

autosyntax turns simple words into to code.
For example:
list milk eggs coffee nutella
becomes >
['milk', 'eggs', 'coffee', 'nutella']



similarily,
dict meaning 42 happy pizza
>
{'meaning': 42, 'happy', 'pizza'}



autosyntax can also do more complicated stuff, like:
class Dog Animal init fluffy True
>
class Dog(Animal):
  def __init__(fluffy=True):
  super().__init__()
